Dr. Rose-Marie Scarlato

Nephrologist & Perioperative Medicine Physician

Dr. Rose-Marie Scarlato is an early-career nephrologist with expertise in general nephrology, hypertension, nephrogeriatrics, and supportive care. She works as a neurologist at St. Vincent's Hospital Sydney and Concord Repatriation General Hospital and as a perioperative medicine physician at Macquarie University Hospital. Dr. Scarlato is also a lecturer for the Faculty of Medicine at University of Sydney. Her clinical practice focuses on providing comprehensive care for patients with chronic kidney disease, end stage renal failure and hypertension, particularly in elderly populations. Dr. Scarlato is also passionate about research, with interests in living kidney donation and addressing gender disparities in academic and clinical medicine. Additionally, she has extended expertise in perioperative and general medicine inpatient care.
