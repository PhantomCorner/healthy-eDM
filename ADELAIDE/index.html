Integrating VAZKEPA® Into CV Risk Reduction Strategies:
Practical Experience and Guidance
Tuesday 20 May 2025 | 7.00pm – 9.00pm
Adelaide Marriott Hotel (Archive Room)
Level 1, 141 King William Street, Adelaide SA 5000
VAZKEPA® is an ethyl ester of eicosapentaenoic acid (EPA), indicated to reduce the risk of cardiovascular (CV) events in high CV risk, statin-treated patients with elevated triglycerides (≥1.7 mmol/L), and is now PBS listed.1,2 Join us for an insightful discussion on triglyceride-associated residual risk and the current landscape of triglyceride management in Australia.
We will explore the science behind VAZKEPA® and key findings from the REDUCE-IT study,3 followed by practical guidance on integrating VAZKEPA® into CV risk reduction strategies. The session will also cover PBS criteria and an interactive real-world case study, providing actionable insights for your clinical practice.
