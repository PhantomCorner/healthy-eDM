Thank you for attending last week's accredited, expert-facilitated, independent educational event, ‘IV Iron Implementation and Sustainable Practice’ . Some of the key takeaways from the speakers’ presentations were:

  • Sustainability is achievable – GP clinics can offer timely, accessible, and cost-effective IV iron therapy, improving patient outcomes.
  • Cost and timely effectiveness of oral iron vs the benefits of IV iron therapy.

When you are considering treatment for appropriate ID/IDA patients, please remember the difference that a single infusion of up to 1,000mg of Ferinject can make.1,2

Compared to oral iron within 30 days 1,000mg of Ferinject showed:3

  • 23g/L increase in haemoglobin
    (14g/L with oral therapy) p<0.001*
  • 92mcg/L increase in ferritin
    (13mcg/L with oral therapy) p<0.001*

*In women with HMB and IDA with 95% compliance to oral 

A single dose of Ferinject effectively corrects iron deficiency¥ in women:2

  • Significantly reduces fatigue^
  • And improves mood and cognitive
    function#
